<p>Uthkar saare kaam karna,<br>
Gharme kisi ka na hona,<br>
Kyu roz aaine mein ek surat si hai anjani,<br>
Yeh hai iski roz ki kahani.</p>

<p>Raftar bhari zindagi mein,<br>
Iski gaadi dheemi si hai,<br>
Local k hazaro chehro mein ek,<br>
Yeh choti iski kahani si hai.</p>

<p>Kaam ki imandari zindagi se isne sikhi hai,<br>
Jaib mein rupae kam hai lekin,<br>
Rah-guzar logo ki kahaniyo mein,<br>
Isne puri duniya dekhi hai.</p>

<p>Dost zyada nahi hai iske,<br>
Dosti par khoob nibhata hai,<br>
Thak haar kar din bhar se,<br>
Lautkar chaar deewaro mein aajata hai.</p>

<p>Aam si zindagi mein,<br>
Khushiyo ki samajh hai isse,<br>
Din kaisa bhi beeta ho,<br>
Dusro ki fikra hai ise.</p>

<p>Kabhi rah mein mil jae yeh saksh,<br>
Toh kar lena do baat purani,<br>
Tumhari muskaan k peeche hi chupi hai,<br>
Iski yeh roz ki kahani.</p>

<p>– Suri</p>
