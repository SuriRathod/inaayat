<p>” kisi din soche toh pata chala,<br>
kahi kho se gaye hai hum,<br>
ek safar par niklo aap bhi kabhi, khudko dhundhne ki chaah liye – Khoj Apni.”</p>

<p>Ek roz baithe aur socha,<br>
kya kare ja rahe hai hum yaha,<br>
dhundhane nikle the khud ko,<br>
khud ko hi bhule ja rahe hai hum yaha.</p>

<p>Kyu itni mayusi hai yaha,<br>
khushiya kahi mehengi toh nahi,<br>
kisi din baazar mein niklo toh samjhe,<br>
ghar chod aai cheez ko dhundhne nikle hai yaha.</p>

<p>Ghar pohoche toh aaina dekha,<br>
aaine mein aaj koi anjaan sa dekha,<br>
kya galat kiye ja raha tha in saalo se mein,<br>
Shayad khudko pheli baar uss aaine mein dekha.</p>

<p>Dhire hi sahi par aakhe toh khuli,<br>
gamo se ghire jaha mein ek roshni toh mili,<br>
ab chala hu na rukne k liye mein,<br>
zindagi jeene ki ek wajah toh mili.</p>

<p>fikar k do pehaloo hai,<br>
ek jisne kaiyo ko dubaya hai,<br>
toh dusra jisne kaiyo ko par lagaya,<br>
hum pehaloo k kisi bhi taraf kyu na ho,<br>
yaad rakhna fikar bass ek pehaloo hai, Pal nahi.</p>

<p>-Suri</p>
