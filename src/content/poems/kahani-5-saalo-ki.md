<p>Aaj ka suraj kuch naya sa hai,<br>
Aakho mein roshni aur chehra khila sa hai,<br>
saalo phele shuru kiya tha jo safar,<br>
Aaj uska anjaam mila sa hai. – ” Kahani 5 Saalo Ki “</p>

<p>Aaye the jab hum yaha,<br>
Ajnabee the tab hum yaha,<br>
waqt k rango mein hum aise dhale,<br>
zindagi ko naya maksad de gaye hum yaha.</p>

<p>Aaine taab bhi dekha karte the,<br>
Aaina aaj bhi dekhte hai,<br>
Dekhte hai hum khudko badalte hue,<br>
Unhi aakho mein nayi umeed dekhte hai.</p>

<p>kitna kuch tha jo na kaha,<br>
kitna kuch tha jo sunn liya,<br>
khush rakhne ki iss duniya mein,<br>
humne humara hi maan rakh liya.</p>

<p>Kabhi bina manzil chal lijiye,<br>
ek kadam hausle ka rakh lijiye,<br>
daar lagna toh jayaj hai yaha,<br>
apne dil ko yeh baat samjha lijiye.</p>

<p>Shuruaat dheemi hui toh kya hua,<br>
Jeb choti hui toh kya hua,<br>
Rutba koi jeb se nahi dekhta galib,<br>
Vasl (Passion) ko paane mein waqt laga toh kya hua.</p>

<p>Raahe alag toh hogi,<br>
woh baate fir na hogi,<br>
dil chota na karna tum yaha,<br>
woh yaade bass tumhari hogi.</p>

<p>– Suri</p>
