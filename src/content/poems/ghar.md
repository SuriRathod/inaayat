<p>Chale the manzil ki khoj mein jo, aaj kahi kho se gae hai,<br>
yaado ki potli se kuch yaade nikal kar, aaj ghar khoj rahe hai – ” Ghar ”</p>

<p>Aksar mein ek baat sochta hu,<br>
kyu mein in khayalo mein rehta hu,<br>
jalta hu apni hi lagai aag mein,<br>
uss aag mein diye(deepak) ki baat sochta hu.</p>

<p>Sach hi kehte hai log,<br>
dur kaab tak rahoge ghar se,<br>
gumrah safar par jaab milenge tumhe log,<br>
toh puchna “kya tum bhi bhaage the apne ghar se?”</p>

<p>Mushkil hai aise jeena,<br>
apne sapno ko roz ek naya bahana dena,<br>
sikha hai humne ek naya tarika,<br>
palko par aasu lekar yaha jeena.</p>

<p>Sikayat nai kar sakte,<br>
palat kar ja bhi nahi sakte,<br>
kis cheez se bandhe hai hum yaha khabar nahi,<br>
ab toh fariyad bhi nahi kar sakte.</p>

<p>kitne bhi dur chale jao,<br>
naye taur-tarike sikh jao,<br>
thak kar jaab aakhe band hoti hai humari,<br>
toh ek khayal aata hai ” chalo ab ghar chale jao.”<br>
-Suri</p>
