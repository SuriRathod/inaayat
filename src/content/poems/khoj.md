<p>“Roshni jo dil ko chu jae,<br>
Usse dilse utarne na dena,<br>
Mile jo tumhe apna humsafar,<br>
Fir kabhi usse khone na dena – Khoj. “</p>

<p>Befikari mein agar khayal ho,<br>
Toh sochna tum jarur,<br>
Beshak milenge tumhe yaha,<br>
Na jaane kitne gurur.</p>

<p>Aihsas koi shabd nahi,<br>
Jisse bayan kar diya jaye,<br>
Umra beet jaati hai logo ki,<br>
Ab toh isse rehne hi diya jaye.</p>

<p>Khudse rubaru hoone ka maan kare,<br>
Toh aaina dekh lena jarur,<br>
Pehchan pao agar khud ko,<br>
Toh mujhe bata dena tum jarur.</p>

<p>Kyu in deewaro mein daba ja raha hu,<br>
Koi jara bata de mujhe,<br>
Yeh kaha mein chala ja raha hu.</p>

<p>– Suri</p>
