<p>Mushkil agar waqt hota,Toh dhairya jaan jaate,<br>
Sahi sawaal agar puchte,<br>
Toh tum jaan jaate.</p>

<p>Toh dhairya jaan jaate,<br>
Sahi sawaal agar puchte,<br>
Toh tum jaan jaate.</p>

<p>Himmat ka ek kadam aage badhate,<br>
Shayad Zindagi ko jaan jaate,<br>
Agar naakam bhi hote Yaha,<br>
Toh galib tajurbe ko jaan jaate.</p>

<p>Aate hai waqt par firse,<br>
Aap Khayalo mein ghum toh nahi jaate,<br>
Haan waqt thoda mushkil zarur hai,<br>
Par aap thoda sa kyu nahi muskurate.</p>

<p>– Suri</p>
