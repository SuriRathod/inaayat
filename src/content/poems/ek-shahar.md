<p>Aakh khulti hai roz subh,<br>
Kya ajeeb yeh sapna lagta hai,<br>
Sapno mein dekha jo Shahar,<br>
Kya sahi mein waisa lagta hai?</p>

<p>Kismat khuli ek subh,<br>
Manzil ka ishara lagta hai,<br>
Sochta raha mein raat bhar,<br>
Woh shahar kaisa lagta hai?</p>

<p>Kuch toh khass hai yaha,<br>
Jo yeh ghar sa lagta hai,<br>
Raat hote hi yeh shahar,<br>
Bilkul sapne jaisa lagta hai.</p>

<p>– Suri</p>
