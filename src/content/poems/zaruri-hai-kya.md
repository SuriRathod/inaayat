<p>Sochta bohot hai tu,<br>
Sochna zaruri hai Kya?<br>
Kai rasme hai iss duniya ki,<br>
Nibhana Zaruri hai Kya?</p>

<p>Ji Raha hai markar bhi,<br>
Aise Jeena zaruri hai Kya?<br>
Sath agar nibhana ho kisika,<br>
Toh sath rehna zaruri hai Kya?</p>

<p>Chahte toh hum bohot kuch hai,<br>
Sab hasil hojae, aisa zaruri hai Kya?<br>
Raasto par kaisi yeh bheed hai,<br>
Bheed mein shamil hona zaruri hai Kya?</p>

<p>Jiss pyaar k liye dard seh Raha hai tu,<br>
Woh pyaar ko paana zaruri hai Kya?<br>
Iss Zindagi mein umeede Kai hai,<br>
Sochle, Marna itna bhi zaruri hai Kya?</p>

<p>– Suri</p>
