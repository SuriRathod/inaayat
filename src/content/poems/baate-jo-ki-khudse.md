<p>Na shaame ho hasin,<br>
Na subah ho khushnuma,<br>
kaha aagae hai hum,<br>
kaha le aaya yeh khumaar – ” Baate Jo Ki Khudse “</p>

<p>Hume toh bahar jaana pasand nahi,<br>
logo se daar nahi lagta,<br>
Bass insaano ki baate hume pasand nahi.</p>

<p>Manzilo ki talash mein aise kho gae hai,<br>
Raahe toh mili,<br>
Magar raaho par kai sath chod gae hai.</p>

<p>Zamane ki ulfat mein Muqaddar (destiny)  ko bhul se gae,<br>
ek seher unse kya mile,<br>
Unki aakho ki chamak mein muqaddar pa gae,</p>

<p>Humare khwabon ki taabir (interpretation) hum hi nahi kar pae,<br>
Lihaz k daayre mein aise kya uljhe,<br>
Khudko khudhi se nahi mila pae.</p>

<p>Hume logo ko hasana behad bhata hai,<br>
dekhi hai duniya jitni bhi,<br>
sabhi ko muskurana bada bhaata hai.</p>

<p>Nikle the hum dilo k baazar mein,<br>
waha garibi kya dekhi,<br>
khud hi neelam hogae.</p>

<p>Hume fakiro se bada lagaav hai,<br>
kisi din unse mile toh jaana,<br>
Ruhaaniyat (soulfulness) bhare maan mein bhi ek ghaav hai.</p>

<p>Yuh toh khudse rubaru  hojae aisa koi waqt nahi,<br>
baate kare bhi toh kisse kare,<br>
Insaano ki baate jo hume pasand nahi.</p>

<p>– Suri</p>
