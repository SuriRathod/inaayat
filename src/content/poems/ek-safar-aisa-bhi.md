<p>“Aksar chote-chote makaan badi badi baatein sikha jaate, kuch pal agar sath bitao toh samjh jaoge unki baate – Ek Safar Aisa Bhi ”</p>

<p>Dur kahi baadal ghire honge,<br>
yaha ek boond ko tarsa koi,<br>
na jaane kitne kharche honge,<br>
yaha do paise ko tarsa koi.</p>

<p>hansi unki,<br>
khel unka,<br>
ek naya andaaz unka,<br>
kisi din bhul jao jeena agar,<br>
toh yaad rakhna tarika unka.</p>

<p>kya hua jo jagah kaam hai,<br>
aakho k bheetar jhako toh aasman chota,<br>
kya hua jo humari deeware purani,<br>
aksar purani cheeze sath dejati.</p>

<p>aaj hui hai barish yaha bhi,<br>
sab sath baith boondo ko nihar rahe,<br>
kuch toh alag baat hai inhme,<br>
jo sab milkar yuh muskura rahe.</p>

<p>raftar si zindagi mein,<br>
do pal jara ruk sa gaya,<br>
jaab bijli giri aur nind khuli,<br>
toh naya nazariya sikh sa gaya.</p>

<p>-Suri</p>
