<p>Zinda toh sabhi hai yaha,<br>
Matlab samajhne ki deri hai,<br>
Girte toh sabhi hai yaha,<br>
Uthkar chalna bohot zaroori hai.</p>

<p>Manzil milegi tumhe yaha,<br>
Gumrah hone ki deri hai,<br>
Dete rahe bahane hazaar,<br>
bass Waqt Milne ki deri hai.</p>

<p>Baithe hai ab hum yaha,<br>
Sabki apni majhburi hai,<br>
Reh gai thi jo kitaab,<br>
Ab kiss baat ki deri hai.</p>

<p>Haste toh sabhi hai yaha,<br>
Dil chune ki deri hai,<br>
Kaisa bhi din ho yaha,<br>
Suno, Muskaan bohot zaroori hai.</p>

<p>– Suri</p>
