<p>Bachpan se judi kai rangeen yaade hai,<br>
unn mein se ek hai rail ka safar,<br>
har ek ki apni kahani hai,<br>
har ek ka apna safar – “Rail – yaado ka safar”</p>

<p>Khidki se chipke baithe hai,<br>
nigahe bahar tiki.<br>
kis umeed mein na jaane baithe hai,<br>
kaab lautegi yeh khushi.</p>

<p>Mausam suhana tha bahar,<br>
barsaat ka sath bhi tha bahar,<br>
na jaane kis khushi se num hui yeh aakhe,<br>
boondo sang aasu bhi aagae bahar.</p>

<p>Sach hi kaha hai kisine,<br>
waqt badalte der nai lagti,<br>
dekh ek choti si muskaan,<br>
laut aagai woh purani hasi.</p>

<p>Na jaane kitne mausam guzre,<br>
kitno ka sath mila hai iss safar mein,<br>
yuh toh waqt nahi hai insaano k liye,<br>
par yaado se milne ka waqt mila hai iss safar mein.</p>

<p>Arsa hogya safar kiye,<br>
bahano ka kila(mehal) tayar hai,<br>
sach kahu toh daar lagta hai kehne mein,<br>
bahano k peeche yaado ka dariya jo tayar hai.<br>
– Suri</p>
