<p>“Har Kahani mein kai raate hoti hai, unn raato se hi toh kahani hoti hai.<br>
Unhi raato mein se nikli hai yeh kahai, kehlati Ek Raat”</p>

<p>Waqt k aane aur jaane se pareh,<br>
kat rahi thi zindagi kuch iss tarah,<br>
Betabiya toh bass maan mein reh gai,<br>
Aisi kati thi woh Ek Raat kuch iss tarah.</p>

<p>Saalo saal ka wada na raha,<br>
Jisse kiya tha wada woh khud na raha,<br>
Wado ki farmaish pesh karta raha woh kuch is tarah,<br>
Aisi kati thi woh Ek Raat kuch iss tarah.</p>

<p>Andhere mein logo ko khojta chala gaya woh,<br>
Ujhale mein apno ko bhulata chala gaya woh,<br>
farak andhere aur ujhale ka samjha usne kuch iss tarah,<br>
Aisi kati thi woh Ek Raat kuch iss tarah.</p>

<p>Samaye se halat puche,<br>
Toh Zindagi ki khabar mili,<br>
Mili thi woh nazar unse kuch iss tarah,<br>
Aisi kati thi woh Ek Raat kuch iss tarah.</p>

<p>Mushkilo ki rah mein umeed toh hogi,<br>
Himmat rakh bande, manzil haasil toh hogi,<br>
Waqt ki deri ko samjha usne kuch iss tarah,<br>
Aisi Kati thi woh Ek Raat kuch iss tarah.</p>

<p>-Suri</p>
