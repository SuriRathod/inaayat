<p>Andhero ki zabaan<br>
Meine samjhi hai aksar,<br>
Ujale mein khudko,<br>
Meine Gumrah paya hai.</p>

<p>Akelepan ka sath,<br>
Meine nibhaya hai aksar,<br>
Logo k beech khudko,<br>
Meine tanaha paya hai.</p>

<p>– Suri</p>
