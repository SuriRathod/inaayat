<p>“Kabhi mile ka aisa kho do jara, toh ek paal rukkar uski yaado mein dekhna,<br>
aksar yaade hi aihsas kara jaati hai unki kimat – Woh aaj fir Laute.”</p>

<p>Bichad gae the kaal jo,<br>
woh aaj fir laute hai,<br>
Guzar gae the paal jo,<br>
woh aaj fir laute hai.</p>

<p>Duniya mein aise uljhe hai,<br>
ki ab suljhan ki aas na bachi,<br>
Bhatke the yaha par jo,<br>
woh aaj fir laute hai.</p>

<p>Umeede aasan thi jitni,<br>
Hakikat mein utna hi zehar bhara,<br>
zehar peekar aasman mein ude the jo,<br>
woh aaj fir laute hai.</p>

<p>Duniya ne apnaya nahi jise,<br>
Khushiyo se milaya nahi jisse,<br>
muskurana sikha gae duniya mein jo,<br>
woh aaj fir laute hai.</p>

<p>ek jaan hi toh hai,<br>
na jaane kaab chali jae,<br>
saaso ka matlab samjha gae the jo,<br>
woh aaj fir laute hai.</p>

<p>-Suri</p>
