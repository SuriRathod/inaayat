<p>“Kisi din ghumne niklo toh duniya nazar-andaaz na karna, diye mein khush rehkar zyada ki fariyaad na karna – Kahani Jo Na Bhuli.”</p>

<p>koshish puri karta hu,<br>
har din ek mannat karta hu,<br>
sochta nahi mein iss duniya ki behrehmi,<br>
bass apne aap se hi shikayat karta hu.</p>

<p>bass mein na tha mere kuch,<br>
bass mein na hai mere kuch,<br>
palke zara si kya zhakpi humne,<br>
humse ruth gaya humara sab kuch.</p>

<p>aasan nahi hai aise jeena,<br>
din dhalte hi umeedo ka khona,<br>
kisi din khali hath ghar jao toh samjhoge humara dard,<br>
kitna mushkil hai aise saas lena.</p>

<p>garibi ek purana khilona hai,<br>
har zubaan se nikla wahi bahana hai.<br>
dekhte toh hai kai log isse,<br>
par logo ka kaam toh bhul jaana hai.</p>

<p>umeedo ki paribhasha koi humse sikhe,<br>
kuch na hokar bhi khush rehna koi humse sikhe,<br>
na jaane kyu udaas hai log chaaro aur,<br>
zindagi ko ek mauka dena koi humse sikhe.<br>
-Suri</p>
