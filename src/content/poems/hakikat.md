<p>“Apne hone ya na hone ka aihsas jo kara jae, aisi hai kuch yaado ki kahani,<br>
sochne ka mil jae aapko mauka, aisi hai kuch aaj ki kahani – Hakikat”</p>

<p>Mushkilo ki raah jaise umar kaid hogai,<br>
Na chahkar bhi halat humari baya hogai,<br>
Kya batae duniya aur uske tariko ko,<br>
Humari toh zindagi hi ek raah hogai.</p>

<p>Kwab kuch tumhare bane,<br>
Kwab kuch humare tutte,<br>
Socha jaab kamyabi ki mehfil mein baithkar,<br>
Daud si zindagi mein na jaane kitne chutte.</p>

<p>Nashsa ek aadat hai,<br>
Koi puche hume ‘humari kya halat hai?’,<br>
Gire honge kai log nashe mein yaha,<br>
Hume toh iss nashe ki maano aadat hai.</p>

<p>Soch rahe honge aap, mein peeta hu,<br>
Ji nahi janab mein jeeta (live) hu,<br>
Apne baare mein aur kya kahu,<br>
Zindagi k gamo k nashe mein peeta hu.</p>

<p>‘Kash‘ ek shabd nahi,<br>
Apne aap mein ek lamha hai,<br>
Jiye hai humne aise kai lamhe,<br>
Bass tumhe iska gulam nahi hona hai.</p>

<p>– Suri</p>
