<p>Deeware chaar hai iski,<br>
Par umeedo ki khidki zarur hai,<br>
Badalte inn mausamo mein,<br>
Iss kamre ki kahani badi mashor hai.</p>

<p>Bade toh sabhi hote hai yaha,<br>
Magar badhapan sabhi mein nahi hota,<br>
bin kuch maange bass sath nibhana,<br>
Aisa mijaaz sabhi ka nahi hota.</p>

<p>Kehne ko toh ek kamra hi sahi,<br>
Iski deewaro par yaado ka pitara hai,<br>
Yaade zara dhundli hi sahi,<br>
Par Inhi ka toh zindagi mein sahara hai.</p>

<p>Chalo ab hassi ki baat ki jae,<br>
Dosto k sath bitayi lamho ki baat ki jae,<br>
Garam chai ho ya ho khushiyo k do jaam,<br>
Nashe mein hi sahi,<br>
par aaj firse uss kamre ki baat ki jae.</p>

<p>– Suri</p>
