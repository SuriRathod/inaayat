<p>Zindagi k safar mein lehro se na darna,<br>
Tutenge hosle yaha kai dafa,<br>
apne irado pe shaq tum kabhi na karna – “Khwahishon ki Naav”</p>

<p>Chaar pal ki hai yeh saase,<br>
fir bhi dekho gumaan itna,<br>
hasna bhul chuke hai hum yaha,<br>
fir bhi dekho lihaaz (Respect) kitna.</p>

<p>Bachpan mein ek baat sikhi thi,<br>
aa rahi hai woh ab kaam,<br>
dilse na lagana kisi cheez ko,<br>
dilke irade toh hue hai nakaam.</p>

<p>Girti boonde jaise sawal kai puche,<br>
jawab ka dariya maano mila hi nahi,<br>
yuh toh hum rehte hai ek sheher mein,<br>
sheher aisa jisme ek kinara bhi nahi.</p>

<p>Aahat sunn utha karte the jo,<br>
aaj unki yaad sone nahi deti,<br>
itne bhi kya dur aagae hum ghar se,<br>
lautne ki fariyaad ab rone nahi deti.</p>

<p>Tinka dar tinka waqt guzarta hai yaha,<br>
udas chehre dekh maan machalta hai yaha,<br>
ab na lautenge is gehre samundar se,<br>
khwahishon ki naav ko paar lagana hai yaha.</p>

<p>– Suri</p>
