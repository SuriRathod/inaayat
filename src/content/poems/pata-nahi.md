<p>Band hai kamroo mein hum,<br>
Kal ka kuch pata nahi,<br>
Kehne ko toh insaan hai hum,<br>
Insaniyat ka pata nahi.</p>

<p>Jab mila hai waqt hume,<br>
“Kya karenge” yeh pata nahi,<br>
Bhaagti is Zindagi mein,<br>
Shayad rukna kabhi sikha nahi.</p>

<p>– Suri</p>
